Institution's Innovation Council (IIC)

About MVGR IIC

MVGR in association with MHRD, Govt. of India established 'MHRD's Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) in the year 2020 to promote different activities related to innovation, IPR, start-up, and entrepreneurship.
